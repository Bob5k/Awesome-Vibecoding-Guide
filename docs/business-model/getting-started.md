### Finding Your First Clients

**Local Networking:**
- Chamber of Commerce
- Business networking groups
- Local Facebook groups
- Community events

**Digital Outreach:**
- LinkedIn local business connections
- Google My Business for local services
- Direct outreach to businesses with poor websites
- Nextdoor and local community apps

**Referrals:**
- Friends and family businesses
- Previous colleagues
- Existing client referrals
- Partner with complementary services

### Marketing Your Services

**Portfolio Building:**
- Offer discounted first sites
- Case studies and before/after
- Speed and performance comparisons
- Client testimonials

**Online Presence:**
- Your own showcase website (eat your own dog food)
- LinkedIn presence and posts
- Local SEO optimization
- Google My Business listing

**Value Demonstration:**
- Free website audits
- Performance comparison tools
- Cost savings calculator
- Educational content

### Sales Process

**Initial Contact:**
- Qualification questions
- Understanding their needs
- Setting expectations
- Pricing discussion

**Proposal & Agreement:**
- Simple proposal template
- Clear scope of work
- Payment terms
- Timeline commitments

**Delivery & Follow-up:**
- Launch process
- Training/handoff
- Asking for referrals
- Staying in touch

### Common Challenges

- Dealing with price objections
- Managing scope creep
- Handling difficult clients
- Balancing multiple projects
- Time management with day job
